My Rewatch Movie

Harry Potter has many sequel but this is my one pick! Because it raises the stakes with the Triwizard Tournament, featuring exciting challenges and magical creatures. It marks a shift towards darker themes, especially with Voldemort's return. The movie blends action, mystery, and emotional moments, showing how the characters grow and face tough decisions while sticking together. For me it's has a marvellous action and not so heavy plot.

My Favorite TV Series

Percy Jackson and the Olympians by Rick Riordan follows 12-year-old Percy Jackson, who discovers he's a demigod and the son of Poseidon. Along with his friends Annabeth and Grover, Percy embarks on thrilling quests to save the world from ancient Greek monsters and gods. Packed with humor, adventure, and lessons about friendship and bravery, this series brings Greek mythology into the modern world in an exciting and relatable way.
